About Me.

A self-proclaimed designer and maker. I enjoy all forms of creation, working digitally or with my hands to create for enjoyment and to solve problems. Following close to a decade in the medical device industry, I decided to work towards a change in career! Firstly this entailed a return to University to study and achieve an MSc in Industrial Digitalisation with Distinction. I am now working at a University, supporting businesses in reducing their carbon footprint. I also contribute to electrochemistry research, utilising 3D Printing technology to produce electrically conductive plastics. I am currently working towards completing a Front-End Web Development Bootcamp to expand my digital skillset and seek a new industry position and get creative in the realm of development!
